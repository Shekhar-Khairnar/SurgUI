# Master file for phases, tasks and subtasks for laparoscopic Hiatal Hernia Procedure
# This task analysis is based on Sankaranarayanan et al.
#Phases
1. Prepate patient
2. Dissection of Hernia sac
3. Crural closure
4. Fundoplication
5. Closure

#Tasks
1-1. Establish pneumoperitoneum
1-2. Trocar placement
1-3. Liver retraction
2-1. Establish access to hernia sac/mediastinum avascular plane
2-2. Dissect task downto right and left crus
2-3. Circumferential dissection
3-1. Assess esophageal length
3-2. Seal short gastric vessel
3-3. Suture with or without mesh
4-1. Mobilze gastric fundus
4-2. Suture to complete fundoplication
5-1. Place suction drain
5-2. Remove trocars
5-3. Close incisions
